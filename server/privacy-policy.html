3. Apple HealthKit Integration

GrowthTrack integrates with Apple HealthKit to read sleep, workout, activity, and nutrition data from your device. We strictly comply with Apple's HealthKit guidelines.

  • HealthKit data is accessed only with your explicit permission and is read-only.
  • HealthKit data is never shared with third parties, used for advertising, or sold.
  • HealthKit data is not stored in iCloud and is only transmitted to our encrypted backend servers for the sole purpose of providing app functionality.
  • You can revoke HealthKit access at any time through your iPhone's Settings > Privacy & Security > Health.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To provide core features such as health logging, personalized recommendations, daily morning briefs, and progress tracking.
  • Product Personalization: To tailor recommendations, insights, and content based on your health data, goals, and activity patterns.
  • Analytics: To understand usage patterns, improve app features, and enhance the overall user experience.
  • Account Management: To authenticate your identity, manage your account, and provide customer support.

5. Data Tracking

Some data, including contact information and location data, may be used for tracking purposes as described in the App Tracking Transparency framework. We will request your permission before enabling any tracking, and you can opt out at any time through your device settings.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • Sensitive credentials (authentication tokens) are stored using encrypted device storage (Expo SecureStore).
  • Health data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored on secure, encrypted backend servers.
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.
  • An offline data queue ensures your logged data is securely cached and synced when connectivity is restored.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal or health data to third parties. Your data may be shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With your consent: When you explicitly choose to share achievements or summaries via the app's share feature.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • Service providers: With trusted service providers who help us operate the app, subject to strict data protection agreements.

8. Data Retention

We retain your health and personal data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time through the app's Account Settings. Upon account deletion, all personal and health data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

GrowthTrack is designed for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

10. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you (available through the app's Data Export feature).
  • Correction: Update or correct your personal information through your profile settings.
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data through Account Settings.
  • Portability: Export your health data in standard formats through the Data Export feature.
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke permissions (HealthKit, location, notifications) at any time through your device settings.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy within the app and updating the "Effective Date" above. Your continued use of GrowthTrack after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
